ABOUT US

The Story of Brij Travels Mathura

Founded in 2009 by Satish Chaudhary, Brij Travels has established itself as the best travel agency in Mathura with a mission to showcase the spiritual and cultural heritage of Braj region to travelers from across India and the world.

As natives of Mathura, we bring unparalleled knowledge and passion to every journey. What began as a small cab service has grown into a comprehensive travel solution offering premium tours, transportation, and guided experiences throughout North India. Our deep understanding of Brij Travels Mathura region sets us apart, allowing us to take you beyond the tourist spots to experience the true essence of these sacred lands.

Our Mission

To provide memorable travel experiences that connect people with the spiritual and cultural heritage of Braj region.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in travel services for Mathura-Vrindavan and North India tourism.
